Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we collect your Email Address and Google Unique ID (UID). We also store the tasks, descriptions, and metadata (dates/priority) that you explicitly create within the application.

2. How We Use Data

Your data is used solely to provide and synchronize your task list across different devices. We do not use your data for marketing, nor do we sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

We use Google Firebase and Firestore to host your information. While we use industry-standard security provided by Google Cloud, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

This app utilizes Google OAuth for authentication. By using Focus by Alex, you are also subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You may delete individual tasks at any time. To request a full account deletion and removal of all data associated with your Google UID from our Firebase database, please email simplifiedgroup10@gmail.com.
